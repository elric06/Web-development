About

The Astrum KM350 USB wired mechanical keyboard
has a full-size 104-key slim profile and a switchable
backlit among the pre-set light effect modes (different
light modes). The brightness and speed with which the
lights change may be adjusted. Three groups of backlit
modes can be customized and recorded. There are 14
different LED backlight effects to choose from. Choose
the one that complements your style and completes the
outfit. The keyboard height may be adjusted using the
tilt legs. Compatible with Windows 7, 8, and 10, as well
as Linux and Chrome OS.
